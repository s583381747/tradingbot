QQQ EMA20 Trend Following — Touch → Bounce → Trigger flow

Entry flow (Long):
1. Touch bar: Low reaches EMA20
2. Bounce bar: Close > touch bar High
3. Trigger bar: High ≥ signal line (touch_high + $0.05)
4. Backtest enters at sig (cyan dashed line)
Problem: 68% of trigger bars open above sig. A real stop-buy fills at Open, not sig. The red shaded area = money the backtest gives away for free.
